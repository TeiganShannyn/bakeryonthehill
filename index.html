Welcome to Bakery on the Hill

Nestled in the heart of Upstate NY, our cozy bakery is run by a passionate team of four, dedicated to bringing you the finest baked goods and a warm, inviting atmosphere. From flaky pastries to our signature cakes, every creation is made with love and the freshest ingredients.

A Place to Relax & Gather

Come for the bakes, stay for the ambiance! Our cozy interior invites you to relax and enjoy, whether it’s a sunny morning or a chilly afternoon. Catch up with friends, read a book, or simply sip your favorite drink and unwind in comfort.

Delicious Treats & Freshy Brewed Drinks

Savor our hand-crafted breads, cookies, muffins, and desserts, perfect for any time of the day. Pair your favorite bake with a cup of freshly brewed coffee, soothing tea, or refreshing cold drinks. Whether you are grabbing a quick bite or enjoying a leisurely treat, we have something for everyone.

Order Online or Visit Us Today!

Love what you see? You can order your favorites online or stop by our bakery to enjoy the full experience. Delicious moments are just a click—or a short drive—away!
